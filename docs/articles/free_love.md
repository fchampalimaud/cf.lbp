# Free Love

## Unstucking Braitenberg's wheels

Braitenberg’s original “vehicles” were creatures of passion — turning toward light, chasing shadows, falling in love with stimuli. But their devotion often led to a problem: they got stuck. 

We depart from the first three creatures. The others are exercises of possibilities, not unlike the LBP.


In Free Love, those early impulses are set loose. Here, the vehicles learn to move on — to adapt, turn away, and recover — transforming simple attraction into sustained, purposeful behavior.
