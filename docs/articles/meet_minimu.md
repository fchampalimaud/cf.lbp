# Meet MinimU

**MinimU** is the Little Brain Project’s embodied agent — a small, wheeled, wireless robot that brings minimalist neural architectures to life.  The robot includes onboard sensors for IMU data, wheel rotation, voltage, and battery level, and can be expanded with additional wireless modules.

## Communication and Control

- **Computer communication:** wireless interface for data exchange and control  
- **Bonsai integration:** programming through the **Bonsai** visual reactive framework, based on **ReactiveX**, enabling real-time sensorimotor processing  
- **Sensors:** IMU, encoders, voltage, and current feedback  
- **Actuation:** differential wheel drive with feedback control  

Although not constrained by it, MinimU is typically programmed in **Bonsai**, allowing behavior to emerge through continuous feedback between sensing and actuation.  
Through MinimU, we study how simple circuits, embodied in motion and perception, can give rise to the first glimmers of adaptive and integrated behavior.
